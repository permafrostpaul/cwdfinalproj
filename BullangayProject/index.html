TELECALL FAQs

Q: Are your courses really free?

A: Yes, all our courses are absolutely free. We believe in making quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of financial barriers.

Q: How do I enroll in a course?

A: Enrolling in our courses is simple! Just visit our website, browse our course catalog, and click on the course you're interested in. From there, you can access the course materials and start learning right away.

Q: Do I need any special equipment or software to take your courses?

A: No, you don't need any special equipment or software. Our courses are designed to be accessible to anyone with an internet connection and a device like a computer, tablet, or smartphone.

Q: Can I take your courses at any time?

A: Yes, you can take our courses at any time that's convenient for you. Since our courses are online, you can access them 24/7 from anywhere in the world.

Q: How long do your courses take to complete?

The duration of our courses varies depending on the topic and level of complexity. Some courses can be completed in a few hours, while others may take several weeks or months.

Q: Do I receive a certificate after completing a course?

A: Yes, we offer certificates of completion for our courses. Once you successfully complete a course, you'll have the option to download or print your certificate as proof of your achievement.

Q: Are your courses suitable for beginners?

A: Absolutely! Our courses cater to learners of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Whether you're just starting out or looking to expand your knowledge, you'll find courses that suit your needs.

Q: Can I access course materials offline?

A: Unfortunately, our course materials are only accessible online. You'll need an internet connection to access the course content and complete any assignments or assessments.

Q: Do you offer live telecall sessions as part of your courses?

A: While we primarily offer self-paced online courses, we may occasionally host live telecall sessions for certain courses. These sessions provide an opportunity for students to interact with instructors and peers in real-time.

Q: How can I provide feedback or ask questions about a course?

A: We welcome feedback and questions from our students! You can reach out to us through our website's contact form or email us directly. We strive to respond to inquiries promptly and ensure the best possible learning experience for our students.
